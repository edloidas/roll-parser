Usage:
  roll-parser [options] [<rolls>]

Options:
  --help  # Print the module options and usage

Arguments:
  rolls   # Whitespace separated list of roll notations.
          Only classic or WoD notations are parsed.
          Can be omitted. In that case, classic d20 roll will be used.
          Classic: 2d20+1
          World of Darkness: 6d10!>6f1

Examples:
  roll-parser 2d20+1
  roll-parser 4d10 5d6-1 d100
  roll-parser 6d10!>6f1
  roll-parser --help
